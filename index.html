Ideal Soup Method Protocol for Consistency in this Study:

Core goal: limited contact time and low pressure — regardless of brewer or ratio.

  • Grind halfway between burr chirp and pour-over coarseness.
  • Tamp puck.
  • Add paper filter on basket bottom and paper filter or metal screen on top of puck.
  • Add water at 4× the coffee dose by mass (4:1 water-to-coffee ratio).
  • Displace air gently to push water through at <1 bar (if possible).
  • Target output ratio: 1:3 (coffee to beverage).

All ratios and methods are welcome — what matters is that your actual parameters are recorded accurately below.
